Top 5 reasons to choose a resin driveway

Permeable, low-maintenance, smart and durable — the top five reasons Yorkshire homeowners choose resin-bound driveways.

Get a free quote Call 07984 469764

Guides · 3 min read

Resin bound has become the surface of choice for thousands of homes. Here’s why.

1. Low maintenance

No weeds in joints, no re-sanding — just an occasional jet-wash. See our maintenance guide.

2. Fully permeable

Rainwater drains straight through, so no puddles and usually no planning permission needed.

3. Looks fantastic

A smooth, seamless finish in a wide choice of natural colours and blends that lifts any frontage.

4. Built to last

Laid on a proper base, it lasts 15–20+ years and carries our 15-year guarantee.

5. Comfortable & safe

Smooth and slip-resistant — pram, bike and wheelchair friendly.
