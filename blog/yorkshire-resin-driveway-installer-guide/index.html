How to choose a resin driveway installer in Yorkshire

Not all resin installers are equal. The questions to ask, the red flags to avoid, and how to make sure your driveway is built to last.

Get a free quote Call 07984 469764

Guides · 5 min read

A resin driveway is only as good as the team that lays it. Here’s how to separate the professionals from the chancers.

Ask about the base

A good installer talks about groundwork before they talk about colour. If they’re vague on excavation depth and sub-base, walk away.

Check insurance & guarantees

Make sure they carry public liability cover (we hold £1,000,000) and offer a written guarantee (ours is up to 15 years on full dig-out resin).

See real work & reviews

Look for genuine photos and verified reviews. We’re rated 5.0★ and much of our work comes from recommendations.

Beware the deposit

Be cautious of anyone demanding a large upfront deposit. We don’t take a deposit before work begins — you pay as the job progresses (see how we work).

Red flags

  • No written quote or vague pricing
  • Pressure to decide on the spot
  • Resin bonded sold as permeable resin bound
  • No proof of past work or insurance
