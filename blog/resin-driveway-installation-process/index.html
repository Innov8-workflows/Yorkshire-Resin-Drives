The resin driveway installation process, step by step

From first survey to finished drive — exactly how a resin-bound driveway is installed, stage by stage, and why each step matters.

Get a free quote Call 07984 469764

Guides · 5 min read

Wondering what actually happens when you book a resin driveway? Here’s the full process, start to finish.

1. Free survey & quote

We visit, measure up, check the existing surface and drainage, discuss colours and give you a fixed written price — no deposit, no obligation.

2. Job pack & scheduling

Once you’re happy, we document the job and book a date that suits you and the weather.

3. Excavation & groundwork

We dig out to the correct depth, install or upgrade drainage where needed, and lay and compact a solid, permeable sub-base — see groundworks.

4. Edgings

We fit clean, solid edgings and borders to contain the surface and frame the drive.

5. Laying the resin

We hand-mix premium UV-stable resin with your chosen aggregate and trowel it to a smooth, seamless finish.

6. Cure & clean-up

Walkable in 4–6 hours, vehicles in 24–48. We clear up and leave your drive spotless. See it in action via our gallery.
