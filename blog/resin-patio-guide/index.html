The complete resin patio guide

Everything you need to know about resin-bound patios — benefits, slip resistance, maintenance and how they compare to slabs and paving.

Get a free quote Call 07984 469764

Guides · 4 min read

Resin isn’t just for driveways. A resin-bound patio gives you a smooth, seamless, low-maintenance outdoor space that’s a pleasure to use.

Why choose a resin patio?

  • Seamless — no joints, no trip points, easy to keep clean
  • Slip-resistant — the textured aggregate grips underfoot, even when wet
  • Permeable — drains freely, so no standing water
  • Weed-free — nowhere for weeds to take hold

Resin patio vs slabs

Slabs and natural stone look beautiful but have joints that need pointing and can harbour weeds and moss. Resin gives a continuous, contemporary finish with far less upkeep.

Great for…

Seating areas, around garden rooms and pool surrounds, and linking spaces with matching resin pathways.

The right base

As with driveways, a lasting patio needs the correct groundwork underneath. See our resin patios service.
