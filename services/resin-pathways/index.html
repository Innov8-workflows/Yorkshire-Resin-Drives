Resin Pathways & Walkways in West & North Yorkshire

Resin pathways give you a smooth, seamless, slip-resistant walkway with no joints to trip on and no weeds to pull. They’re ideal for front approaches, side returns and garden paths — and can be laid to match your driveway and patio.

Fully permeable, they drain freely and keep their colour year after year.
