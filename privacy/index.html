Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with us. We keep it simple and we never sell your data.

This privacy policy explains how Yorkshire Resin Drives (“we”, “us”) collects and uses the personal information you provide through this website. Last updated 2026-06-24.

What we collect

When you contact us through our enquiry form, by phone or by email, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, postcode or town and any project details you give us. We only ask for what we need to respond and provide a quote.

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange a site visit or quote
  • To carry out and manage any work you ask us to do
  • To contact you about your project

We will never sell or rent your information, and we won’t use your details for marketing unless you’ve asked us to.

How your enquiry is sent

Our contact form sends your message to us by email so we can reply. The form may use a secure third-party form service to deliver that email. Your details are used only to contact you about your enquiry.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry and customer information only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet our legal and accounting obligations, then securely delete it.

Your rights

You can ask what personal information we hold about you, have it corrected, or have it deleted. To make a request, contact us using the details below.
